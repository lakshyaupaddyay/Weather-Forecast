Usage Guide

Welcome to our weather app! Here's a quick guide on how to use it:

  • Enter the name of the city you want to see the weather for in the search box at the top of the page.
  • Click on the "Search" button or hit the Enter key on your keyboard.
  • The weather for the city you entered will be displayed, including the current temperature, the high and low for the day, and a brief description of the weather conditions.
  • You can also see the sun rise and sun set time .

That's it! We hope you find our weather app useful. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
